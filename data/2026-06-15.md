Abstract: Focused helium ion beam (He-FIB) irradiation with 30 keV ions is a key tool for nanoscale patterning and defect engineering in high transition temperature (Tc) cuprate superconducting devices, yet its usable fluence window is constrained by the competing requirements of reliable superconductivity suppression and minimal structural degradation.
